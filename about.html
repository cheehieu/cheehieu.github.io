#whatis niftyhedgehog

I have some bad news. Unfortunately, this website wasn't created for hedgehogs or hedgehog enthusiasts... #9785 It's actually just a personal website to document my projects, thoughts, and experiences. Truth is, I've had very little interaction with hedgehogs in the real world.

The domain name originates from my childhood fascination with Sonic the Hedgehog. As a youngster, I spent a lot of time reading his comic books and playing the video games; running fast and collecting gold rings were my daily priorities... and in some ways, remnants of those priorities still remain today.

This website has been long overdue, and I'm glad to finally have something up and running. NiftyHedgehog is a minimalist approach to web-presence with almost no financial cost involved.
