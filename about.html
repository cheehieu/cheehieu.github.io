#man --whatis niftyhedgehog

This has been extrememly overdue--a minimalist approach. NiftyHedgehog is an initiative to increase my web-presence while documenting personal projects, thoughts, and experiences. I wish I could say that I'm a hedgehog enthusiast, but the truth is I've had very little interaction with hedgehogs in the real world. ☹ However, the virtual world is a different story. I grew up reading and playing "Sonic the Hedgehog" comics and video games; running fast, spinning, and collecting gold were my daily priorities. In some ways, remnants of those priorities still remain today.

Anyway, I wanted to create a unique domain name for my personal website. My given name, Hieu Nguyen, is far too common and leaves no room for differentiation. So I settled on a combination of words, which could abbreviate to my initials (HMN). And with this obscure naming methodology, NiftyHedgehog was born.

The website is setup to incur minimal cost. Hosted entirely on GitHub, email service through Zoho Mail, built with a responsive Bootstrap framework, projects through GitHub pages, blog through Wordpress, community through Disqus, Google Analytics, monetization with ChangeTip digital currency.
