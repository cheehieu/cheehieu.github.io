I'm not a very good speaker. I don't thrive in high-pressure situations where I need to quickly articulate thoughts into cohesive diction. The English language is a work of art, and unfortunately, I am not a speed painter. I prefer to choose my words carefully, so that my ideas are communicated more accurately and effectively.

This blog will feature random ramblings about life and philosophy, with a mix of science and technology. Some posts will be informative drivel, while others will be outbursts of creative writing. I have a voice, and I'd like to share it with others. My goal is to create at least one post per month. I think that's doable.
